Overview

For instance, During "Covid Times", People had to stay at their homes for majority of months. During that time the items that were supposed to be working gave up and were totally useless for them. While these things had to pile up, people were afraid that these items while neither sell nor be used. Inspired by famous TV show "PAWN STARS", We are starting an online rag picking service where we buy these items from the people and process them further to later create biodegradable items that can be used by the people for the longest while we also provide employement opportunities to the people working with us and an income source to people who are selling their items.

Our work has taken the following measures to manage these scraps here:

  • We have a special storage house in one of its wards to collect scraps for processing.
  • We hire rag pickers to separate these scraps.
  • We use different vehicles to handle different types of scraps. They are covered so as to avoid spillage.
  • The authority also offers other ways to dispose of non-degradable scraps using scientific methods.
